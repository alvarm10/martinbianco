How to surf the course using the summary?

You can surf through the course using the index on the left, or the buttons on the footer.

Click on each heading or subheading of the index to jump to the appropriate page. The subheadings are initially hidden but will drop down when you click on the heading under which they are listed. When the contents of a new subject are displayed, the subheadings of the previous active subject are hidden.

On small devices (Tablets o Smartphones) the menu will be hidden while the contents of the course are being displayed. To display the menu click on the upper right-hand corner icon. Whenever you click on a heading or subheading, the menu will be hidden automatically and the corresponding content will be displayed.

How to surf the course using the footer buttons?

ou can surf through the course using the index on the left, or the buttons on the footer.

Click on the buttons to go to the next or to the previous page. The current page number is shown between those buttons.

How to gain access to the course controls?

In the footer of the screen you can also access the following course controls, amongst others: sound off, enter help screen, increase or reduce font size and download files. The number of control options may vary between courses..

How do the picture carousels work?

To access the pictures in a carousel you can click on the < and > icons on top of the picture to the left and right. You can also go straight to a specific picture by clicking on the circles at the upper part of the picture.

How do the flash cards work?

If you click on any part of the flash card it will rotate and show its contents. Click it again to make the card turn round again and hide its contents. In some cases there will be a sound effect when the card turns.

How do the dynamic pictures work?

The dynamic pictures are groups of pictures of a fixed size with an associated hidden content. If you click on each picture its content will be displayed. Sometimes when you click a picture, a sound file will be automatically activated.

How do the expandable pictures work?

CIf you click on a picture that bears the instruction “Click on the image below”, the expanded view will be activated. If the icons < and > are superimposed on the picture, you can click on them to scroll through to the next pictures. To close the expanded view click on ✖ or anywhere on the screen outside the expanded picture.

How do the drop-down resources work?

Each drop-down resource has clickable areas or buttons. If you click on each one the appropriate information will be displayed and the rest will be hidden. The area or button of the drop-down content being displayed will be shown in a different colour.

How do the time lines work?

The time lines show chronological events. Click on each date to show the associated content. Move along the vertical time line by scrolling with the wheel on your mouse. Move along the horizontal time line by clicking on the buttons on the left or right of the line.

How do the audio controls work?

Click on the first triangular icon o the left to start listening. Click again to pause. The central bar allows you to start listening from a specific point. The duration of the audio is shown on the right. The audio volume will depend on the main volume on your device.

How do the activities work?

Throughout the unit you will find activities that you can complete to test yourself and consolidate the knowledge you have gained. There are several types of activity. Each one has its own instructions. Most of them have an “Answers” button so you can solve the activity. The “Correct” button will show you which option or options are correct and which are incorrect. Depending on the activity, you will see messages giving explanations or comments for each answer chosen.
